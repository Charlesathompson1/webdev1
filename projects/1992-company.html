We are a virtual art gallery that caters to poc artist more specifically black and latina woman. Our mission is to give these woman the space to share their work.

Since 1992 Our company has been ran by woman with a POC background. Running it flawlessly might I add, In the early stages of our company we sourced POC coders in order to make the immersive virtual space you are now in. We are constantly growing and adding to our gallery as more artist within ur community emerge and is excited about improving our audince experience on our sight.
